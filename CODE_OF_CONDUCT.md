# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being respectful and constructive.
- Accepting feedback gracefully.
- Prioritizing user safety and reliability.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberate disruption of project collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may take fair corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Reporting

Report incidents to repository maintainers through private channels listed in SECURITY.md.
